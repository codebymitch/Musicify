# Musicify Trademark & Brand Policy

This document describes how the **Musicify** project’s trademarks and brand assets may be used.

This policy covers the brand identifiers of the project, including but not limited to:
- Any Musicify logos, icons, wordmarks, or other graphics
- Musicify’s bot profile imagery (avatar/banner), theme, and other distinctive visual identity
- The Musicify website/domain names and social media identifiers
- Any other names, slogans, or design elements that identify Musicify as a product/service

This trademark policy is **separate** from the copyright license that applies to the source code.
Even if the source code is available under an open-source or source-available license, you **do not automatically receive rights** to use the Musicify trademarks.

## 1. Ownership

All Musicify trademarks and brand assets are owned by the Musicify project maintainers (the “Owners”).
All goodwill associated with use of the Musicify trademarks inures to the benefit of the Owners.

## 2. Permitted Uses (No Permission Needed)

You may use the Musicify name **only as necessary** to make truthful statements about the project, including:

1. **Referential / nominative use**
   - You may say “Musicify is a Discord music bot” or “Compatible with Musicify.”
   - You may link to the official repository and website.
   - You may use “Musicify” in blog posts, reviews, tutorials, comparisons, and documentation, provided it is clear you are referring to the official project and not implying endorsement.

2. **Forks for private/internal use**
   - You may fork the repository and run modified versions privately, provided you do **not** use Musicify branding in a way that suggests your fork is the official Musicify service.

3. **Accurate attribution**
   - You may state that your work is “based on Musicify” or “forked from Musicify,” so long as you also comply with the “Fork naming rules” below.

## 3. Restricted Uses (Permission Required)

You must obtain **prior written permission** from the Owners to:

- Use the Musicify logo in the name of a product, service, bot listing, server listing, domain name, or social media account.
- Use Musicify branding (name/logo/visual identity) in a way that suggests your project is the official Musicify bot, is endorsed by Musicify, or is affiliated with Musicify when it is not.
- Use Musicify logos or other brand assets on merchandise, advertisements, sponsorships, or promotional materials.
- Register or use any confusingly similar names or marks (including misspellings, look-alike domains, or similar logos).

## 4. Fork Naming and Branding Rules

If you create a fork or derivative project that you distribute, publicize, or make available to others:

- **Do not** name your fork “Musicify” or anything confusingly similar (e.g., “Musicify2”, “Musicify+”, “Musicify Premium”, “Musicify Pro”, “Musiqify”).
- **Do not** use Musicify logos, icons, or bot profile imagery.
- Choose a distinct name and visual identity.
- You may describe your project as:
  - “A fork of Musicify” or “Based on Musicify”
  - “Derived from the Musicify codebase”
  but you must not imply it is the official Musicify bot.

## 5. Acceptable Use Examples

**Allowed:**
- “This feature is inspired by Musicify.”
- “Forked from Musicify (link to original).”
- “Musicify is licensed under <CODE LICENSE>; trademarks are not included.”

**Not allowed (without permission):**
- Running a public bot called “Musicify” (or similar) on Discord.
- Using the Musicify logo as your bot avatar.
- Hosting “musicify-bot.com” or “musicifydiscord.net”.
- Listing your bot publicly in a way that implies it is the official Musicify service.

## 6. Non-endorsement

Any use of the Musicify name must not imply endorsement, sponsorship, or affiliation unless you have written permission.
If you are unsure, include a clear disclaimer such as:

> “This project is not affiliated with or endorsed by Musicify.”

## 7. Reporting Misuse

If you believe the Musicify trademarks are being misused, please contact:
- **GitHub:** open an issue in the repository with links and evidence

## 98 Changes to This Policy

This policy may be updated over time. Continued use of Musicify trademarks must follow the latest version of this policy.
